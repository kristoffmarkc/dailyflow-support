## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add a task?
Tap the **+** button at the bottom right of the screen. A new empty task will appear â€” type your task and press Done on the keyboard.

### How do I complete a task?
Tap the circle next to any task to mark it as complete. Completed tasks show a green checkmark and strikethrough text.

### How do I delete a task?
Swipe left on any task to reveal the delete button, then tap it. You can also swipe all the way left to delete immediately.

### How do I edit a task?
Tap directly on the task text to enter edit mode. Make your changes and press Done.

### How do I view past tasks?
Tap the **Today** button (or date button) in the header to open the calendar. Select any date with a grey indicator to view archived notes from that day.

### How do I create multiple lists?
This is a Premium feature. Open the menu (hamburger icon), tap **Lists**, then tap **New List** to create additional task lists.

### How do I use the Diary?
This is a Premium feature. Open the menu and tap **Diary** to switch to diary mode where you can write daily journal entries.

### How do I change the theme?
Open the menu and find the **Theme** toggle. Free users can use System mode (follows iOS setting). Premium users can manually select Light or Dark mode.

### Is my data backed up?
Your data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your app data will be included in your device backup.

### Does DailyFlow collect my data?
No. DailyFlow does not collect any personal data. All your tasks and diary entries remain on your device. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy.md) for details.

### How do I restore my Premium purchase?
If you've previously purchased Premium on another device or after reinstalling, open the menu and tap **Restore Purchases**.

### What is the "Start Fresh" popup?
Each day when you first open DailyFlow, you'll see a prompt to create a new task list for the day. This helps you start each day with a fresh, organized mindset.

### What is Flow Mode?
Flow Mode is a minimalist free-writing space for capturing thoughts, ideas, or stream-of-consciousness writing. It's designed for unstructured creativity without the constraints of tasks or formatting. Access it from the menu (Premium feature).

### What is the Year Map?
The Year Map is a 365-dot visualization of the year. Past days are shown in a distinct color, serving as a visual reminder of how quickly time passes. This feature is designed to inspire you to stay focused and make the most of each day. Access it by tapping the "Day/365" button in Flow Mode.

### How do I undo changes?
Shake your device to reveal the Undo card. This works in Tasks, Diary, and Flow modes. Tap the "Undo" button to revert your last action.
