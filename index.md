# DailyFlow Support

## About DailyFlow

DailyFlow is a beautiful, minimalist daily task management app for iOS. Stay organized with daily tasks, track your progress, and never lose sight of what needs to be done.

## Features

- ✅ **Daily Task Management** — Add, edit, and complete tasks with ease
- ✅ **Multiple Lists** — Organize tasks into different lists
- ✅ **Progress Tracking** — Visual progress bar shows your daily completion
- ✅ **Archive System** — Browse past notes by date
- ✅ **Dark Mode** — Beautiful dark and light themes
- ✅ **Localization** — Available in 12 languages
- ✅ **Privacy First** — All data stays on your device

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add a task?
Tap the **+** button at the bottom right of the screen, type your task, and press Done.

### How do I complete a task?
Tap the circle next to any task to mark it as complete.

### How do I delete a task?
Swipe left on any task to reveal the delete option.

### How do I view past tasks?
Tap the calendar button in the header to browse archived notes by date.

### Is my data backed up?
Your data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your app data will be included in your device backup.

### Does DailyFlow collect my data?
No. DailyFlow does not collect any personal data. All your tasks remain on your device. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy.md) for details.
